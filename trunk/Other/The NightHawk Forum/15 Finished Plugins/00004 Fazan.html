Fazan
« on: March 07, 2007, 10:33:35 PM »
Fazan is a game of words, each player has to say a word that begins with last 2 letters of previous said word.
The project can be found here: http://sourceforge.net/projects/fazan , and it includes the hexhub plugin implementation of romanian (using the DEX dictionary) and english (using the Webster dictionary) games.
