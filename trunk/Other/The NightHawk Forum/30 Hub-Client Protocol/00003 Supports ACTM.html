Supports ACTM
« on: October 05, 2007, 09:49:45 PM »
ACTM is a replacement for the current $ConnectToMe and $RevConnectToMe commands/syntici.
This feature must be signalled in the Supports string.
The command names have been shorted to $CTM and $RCTM respectively.
Clients supporting ACTM must reply to incoming $ConnectToMe and $RevConnectToMe requests, but will themselves always send $CTM or $RCTM
Complete syntax:
Active
QuoteClient1->Hub:  $CTM <Client2 Nick>$<local port>$<4-digit ID>|
Hub->Client2:  $CTM <Client1 IP>$<Client1 Port>$<4-digit ID>|
Passive
QuoteClient1->Hub:  $RCTM <Client2 Nick>|
Hub->Client2:  $RCTM <Client1 Nick>|
Client2->Hub:  $CTM <Client1 Nick>$<local port>$<4-digit ID>|
Hub->Client1:  $CTM <Client2 IP>$<Client2 Port>$<4-digit ID>|
For checking the 4-digit ID, see Support ACTM
under Client-Client protocol
