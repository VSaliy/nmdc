$MyPass
« on: May 02, 2007, 09:16:53 PM »

Syntax
Quote$MyPass <password>|
Description
• Command sent in response to a $GetPass.
• <password> is the plain text version of your password.
• The pipe (|) and dollar sign ($) in passwords will probably break many clients and hubs.
