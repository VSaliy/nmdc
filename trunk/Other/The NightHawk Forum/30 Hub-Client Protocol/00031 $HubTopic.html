$HubTopic
« on: May 02, 2007, 09:39:17 PM »

The $HubTopic command sends the topic of the hub to the client, similar to IRC
Syntax
Quote$HubTopic <topic>|• <topic> is a string.
Availability
The following clients support $HubTopic:
• iDC++ >= 2.0
• Valknut >= 0.3.0
• StrongDC++ >= 2.02
The following servers support $HubTopic:
• Verlihub
• HexHub
