$CTM
« on: October 05, 2007, 09:52:38 PM »
Indicates an active connection request according following ACTM support
complete syntax
QuoteClient1->Hub:  $CTM <Client2 Nick>$<local port>$<4-digit ID>|
Hub->Client2:  $CTM <Client1 IP>$<Client1 Port>$<4-digit ID>|
For more information, see Support ACTM
