$BotList
« on: May 02, 2007, 09:51:54 PM »

Syntax
Quote$BotList <bot1>$$<bot2>$$<bot3>$$...|
Description
This command is used by a hub to transmit a list of all Bots to a client. This list is delimited by the "$$" token. BDCH, BlackDC and ZionPlusPlus support this feature to distinguish between operators and bots.
