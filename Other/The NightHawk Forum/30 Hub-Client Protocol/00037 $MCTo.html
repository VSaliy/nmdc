$MCTo
« on: May 02, 2007, 09:22:14 PM »

Syntax
Quote$MCTo: <target_nick> $<sender_nick]> <message>|
Description
$MCTo is a private-message to a single user that is displayed as an ordinary chat-message.
• <target_nick> is the peer who should receive the message.
• <sender_nick> is the sender''s nick.
• <message> is the text of the message (spaces permitted).
