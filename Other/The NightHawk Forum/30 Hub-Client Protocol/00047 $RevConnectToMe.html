$RevConnectToMe
« on: May 02, 2007, 09:09:14 PM »

Syntax
Quote$RevConnectToMe <ClientNick> <TargetNick>|
Description
A client sends this command to indicate that it wants <TargetNick> to initiate a $ConnectToMe to it. This is used when the client cannot accept inbound connections (i.e. is passive).
